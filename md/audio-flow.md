Key:
    esx2    Elextribe Sampler 2
    pulse2  Waldorf Pulse 2
    dr-40   Tascam DR-40
    mix100  Tapco Mix 100

-----
Set Up A:

esx2    --midi-out->    pulse2
esx2    --stereo->      mix100
pulse2  --stereo->      mix100
